02/24/2002:

A new development version has been released. This will hopefully resolve some of the subtle synchronization issues in the 1.5.X series of releases. It also is a testbed for improvements and enhancements for the library. This should be pretty stable right now, and perhaps more useful that the 1.5.X series, so don't be hesitant to give it a shot.

Earlier, I looked through and examined the libraries implementation. I discovered some potential sources of subtle problems with the 1.5.X series implementation. I've also solved most of those problems. If time permits, I can hopefully write a short article describing this in more detail.
